Description

Cosmic Water Lady is an effort to represent the sacred female ideal. Floating in space a literal floating godhead in shape of a gigantic woman with blue hair made of holy water. She is worshiped by an alien civilization because of her awe inspiring magnificence , a temple is erected in front of her on the small planet, to celebrate her awe inspiring charm and beauty.

Around her head are different objects, this was inspired by Hindu deities holding symbolic objects in their hands. The crescent moon and pyramids represent the astonishing sense of awe felt encountering this energy. The mushroom, ankh and scales represent the vital flow of life and nature, and the divine justice governing it. The Starfish is a representation of the element of water and the mysterious feel of an ocean which the female figure contains. Saturn and Jupiter aligning is representing the sense of balance that is provided while this energy is flowing freely.

Details

The drawing was drawn in an improvisational manner, subconsciously drawn with no prior planning.

  • The art style is flat, incorporating a mixture of modern and realistic cartoon styles.

  • The color palette is a mix of vibrant colors used to create strong contrast with the dark background.

  • The piece was created using a Wacom Intuos Draw drawing tablet, 100 percent digitally.

  • It was minted on Opensea.com as the genesis piece of the Razmard collection.

  • 5 editions were minted and the initial price was listed as 0.033 ETH.
