Description

Immortal tries to enrapture the sense of peace and tranquility felt while meditating. The main subject is an avatar of Lord Shiva a Hindu primal god, which is told to be the inventor of yoga and meditation. His Chakras are represented by the colorful orbs in front of him which are the colors of the rainbow, they are getting balanced as an act of meditation. Two lotus flowers are seen in front with ganja leaves, representing the enlightening properties of these two medicine plants, used since ancient times to reach heightened states of consciousness. The cow and serpent are two mythical animals appearing in most ancient mythologies, one representing a source of sustenance, the other wisdom attained through hardship. There is a Nazar orb on top of the deity's head which is an ancient talisman used in eastern cultures to ward off bad energies, the traditional white circle replaced with a triangle to represent the three higher Chakras that aspire to the divinity. On the right is a space shuttle representing the path of science and on the left falling starts representing the path of inner wisdom and mysticism. Mount Kailash is shown behind Shiva which is one of his symbols, the sky is a gradient made of different lines of colors, which shows how the experienced reality is just a veil if you look close enough.

Details

The drawing was made in an improvisational manner, after a transcendent experience of mine seeing Shiva in a state of meditation.The color palette is inspired by nature and wants to encapture the rejuvenation of water mixed with the sturdiness with the skies in the mostly blue palette. The piece was created using a Wacom Intuos Draw drawing tablet, 100 percent digitally. It was minted on Opensea.com as the second piece of the Razmard collection. 5 editions were minted and the initial price was listed as 0.033 ETH.
